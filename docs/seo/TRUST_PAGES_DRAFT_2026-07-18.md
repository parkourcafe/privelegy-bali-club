## About Other Bali

### SEO title

About Other Bali — A Resident-Curated Bali Guide

### Meta description

Learn how Other Bali selects useful places across the island, keeps practical
details current and separates editorial judgment from paid relationships.

### H1

About Other Bali

### Draft copy

Other Bali is a resident-curated decision guide for choosing the right Bali
place for the moment you are in. Instead of ranking every venue by anonymous
stars, we explain why a place belongs in the guide, who it suits, what to expect
and which practical action to take next.

Travellers use Other Bali for free. A venue cannot buy a better editorial
position. Where a venue relationship or confirmed offer exists, it must be
clearly identified and does not replace the editorial fit decision.

Other Bali does not operate restaurant bookings, delivery, maps or payments.
When a traveller chooses an action, the request is handed to the venue or the
named specialist provider.

## Methodology

### SEO title

How Other Bali Selects Places — Editorial Methodology

### Meta description

See how Other Bali researches, selects, verifies and updates Bali places, menus,
actions and practical recommendations.

### H1

How we select and check places

### Draft copy

Every Other Bali page starts with a traveller decision: the district, moment,
group, budget or practical need the page must answer. Inclusion is based on fit
and usefulness, not on a purchased ranking.

We separate three kinds of information:

1. **Editorial judgment** — why a place is here, who it suits and who may prefer
   somewhere else.
2. **Venue facts** — location, menu, price, hours and available actions, checked
   against official sources wherever possible.
3. **Provider fulfilment** — maps, reservations, delivery and messaging handled
   by the venue or named provider after the traveller leaves Other Bali.

Official-source facts record a source and capture date. If an official source
is unavailable or ambiguous, we do not invent the missing detail. Owner
confirmation is recorded separately from operator review and publication.

Photos require a documented rights or consent record before public publication.
Ratings, anonymous review aggregates and paid ranking positions do not determine
editorial order.

## Corrections

### SEO title

Corrections and Updates — Other Bali

### Meta description

Report a changed menu, closed venue, incorrect detail or rights concern to the
Other Bali editorial team.

### H1

Corrections and updates

### Draft copy

Restaurants and neighbourhoods change quickly. If you find an incorrect address,
changed menu, unavailable action, closed venue, outdated description or photo
rights concern, email `support@otherbali.com`.

Please include the page URL, the detail that needs correction, an official
source where available, and your relationship to the venue or content. Do not
send guest personal data.

We record the report, check the evidence and update or unpublish the affected
fact when required. Editorial opinion is reviewed separately from factual
corrections. A venue may correct its own operational facts but cannot purchase
or directly publish Other Bali's editorial judgment.

Three fastest-to-launch touchpoints:
1. **WhatsApp-first transfer operators (Bali Nusa Transport, Get Driver Bali, Bali Midori)** — published direct contacts, owner-level decisions, and a 45–90 min captive DPS→Canggu ride where a "what to do tonight" handoff is genuinely useful.
2. **Bikago scooter delivery** — one company decision puts a touchpoint into every bike handover (including airport deliveries), with no venue-by-venue negotiation.
3. **Tropical Nomad + Nirvana Life community events** — coworking/wellness clubs actively run member events; co-hosting one is a permissioned, zero-print way to reach the exact Canggu independent-traveller audience within days.
Airport in-terminal media (InJourney tender/agencies) is the highest-reach but slowest and priced-for-brands route — sequence it last.

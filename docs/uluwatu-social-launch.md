## Instagram — 6 Reel scripts

Format: 15–30s vertical, founder-shot. Voice: practical, resident, honest
about trade-offs. Every reel ends on the same CTA frame: logo + "The full
verified guide — link in bio" + page URL.

### R1 — "Uluwatu is not walkable" (pillar)
- **Hook (0–2s, on-screen):** "Uluwatu mistake #1: booking dinner 25 minutes
  from your sunset."
- **Shots:** scooter POV on a cliff lane → map zoom-out sketch (5 zones) →
  golden-hour cliff wide.
- **On-screen beats:** "5 micro-areas" / "one zone per half-day" / "we mapped
  all 24 verified places".
- **Caption:** Uluwatu looks small on the map and drives big. We split it
  into 5 zones and verified 24 places to eat, drink and watch the sun go
  down — so you plan by area, not by vibes. Full guide in bio.
- **CTA/URL:** `/uluwatu` · utm_content=reel-pillar

### R2 — "The 6am bakery" (brunch)
- **Hook:** "Who's awake at 6am in Uluwatu? The baker."
- **Shots:** dawn light on Labuan Sait → pastry case → coffee pour → surfboard
  on scooter passing.
- **Beats:** "opens ~6:00 · closed Mondays" / "pumpkin-seed latte" / "6 more
  verified breakfast spots in the guide".
- **Caption:** Dawn patrol runs on Son of a Baker and BGS. The brunch guide
  ranks all seven verified morning spots by the decision — coffee-first,
  post-surf plates, quiet gardens. Link in bio.
- **CTA/URL:** `/uluwatu/best-brunch` · utm_content=reel-brunch

### R3 — "Beach access: the truth" (sunset/clubs)
- **Hook:** "Half of Uluwatu's 'beach clubs' have no beach."
- **Shots:** cliff-edge pool pan → funicular going down → toes-in-sand at
  Melasti.
- **Beats:** "cliff club ≠ beach club" / "3 have real sand" / "1 is 18+" /
  "we compared all 7".
- **Caption:** El Kabrón: no sand, all drama. Sundays: a funicular to a
  private cove. Tropical Temptation: 18+ by policy. The comparison table
  settles it — beach access, family fit, booking model. Link in bio.
- **CTA/URL:** `/uluwatu/beach-clubs-sunset` · utm_content=reel-clubs

### R4 — "Date night ≠ group night" (date night)
- **Hook:** "Booked a 'romantic' dinner. Got a party of nine at the next
  table."
- **Shots:** candle-lit table two-top → loud sharing-table energy (contrast)
  → quiet garden room.
- **Beats:** "quiet rooms" / "view rooms" / "food-led rooms" / "and the ones
  that are secretly group venues".
- **Caption:** Some famous Uluwatu rooms are brilliant — for eight people.
  The date-night guide separates quiet-conversation tables from
  view-does-the-work tables from actual party rooms. Link in bio.
- **CTA/URL:** `/uluwatu/date-night-restaurants` · utm_content=reel-date

### R5 — "48 hours, zero backtracking" (48h)
- **Hook:** "Two days in Uluwatu without crossing the Bukit twice."
- **Shots:** fast-cut day sequence: coffee → beach → sunset rail → dinner
  fire → next-morning bakery → funicular → bonfire.
- **Beats:** "Day 1: west cliffs" / "Day 2: Bingin → Melasti" / "booking
  notes included" / "rain plan included".
- **Caption:** The classic mistake is treating Uluwatu like one place. Our
  48-hour plan gives each day one side of the peninsula — with the four
  bookings that actually matter. Free, verified, in bio.
- **CTA/URL:** `/uluwatu/48-hours` · utm_content=reel-48h

### R6 — "How we verify" (trust/brand)
- **Hook:** "We don't publish opening hours we can't prove."
- **Shots:** screen-record of a venue page scrolling to "Information last
  checked" → evidence ledger table glimpse → founder walking into a venue.
- **Beats:** "every fact has a source + date" / "no paid ranking, ever" /
  "24 published, 1 held back".
- **Caption:** One Uluwatu venue didn't make our guide — its identity

  fact carries a source and a checked-date. That's Other Bali.
- **CTA/URL:** `/uluwatu` · utm_content=reel-trust

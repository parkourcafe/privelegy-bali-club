# Other Bali — Villa partnership film · script (final)

**Purpose:** on `/villas`, tell the story of what Other Bali is and how the villa
partnership works — the essence of the product, why the two sides are useful to
each other, and the principle that travellers are never routed through a third
party. Sells an ecosystem (a direct channel + a guest tool for the villa; in-villa
recommendation for Other Bali), not a listing.
**Audience:** villa owners, managers and boutique-stay operators in Bali.
**Style:** mixed-media collage (non-photoreal), warm editorial — cream /
terracotta / teal / ink-brown, one locked style key.
**Voice:** warm, clear female narrator (Maya).
**Languages shipped:** English **and** Bahasa Indonesia (shared visuals; audio +
burned subtitles per language).
**Aspect / length:** 16:9 landscape · eight 10-second beats (~75–80s) so every
line breathes.

On-screen text: AI video can't render clean lettering, so **all words are carried
by clean burned subtitles** synced to the voiceover. The punch lines ("free for
travellers", "free for early villa partners", "no commissions, no middlemen") are
spoken, so they land as crisp captions — no garbled AI text.

Guardrails honoured: no tourist payments, no marketplace/checkout, no "booking
volume" guarantee, no invented venue. Other Bali owns the decision, explanation
and trusted link; the villa owns hosting, booking, payment and the guest
relationship.

## Voiceover — English (8 beats)

1. Other Bali is a resident-curated guide to the island — the right place for the
   moment you're in, chosen and explained by people who actually live here.
2. No scraped reviews. No paid rankings. No sponsored lists. Just honest local
   recommendations travellers can trust — and for travellers, it's always free.
3. When someone discovers your villa through Other Bali, they go straight to you —
   your website, your WhatsApp, your booking page. No marketplace, no middleman,
   no commission.
4. Your villa gets its own beautifully presented page — your photos, your story,
   your direct links, and a personalised Bali guide your guests will actually use
   throughout their stay.
5. In return, you simply introduce your guests to Other Bali — add our link to
   your website, mention us on Instagram, or place our QR code on your welcome
   card.
6. Together, we help travellers discover a better Bali — you send us your guests,
   and we send travellers straight to you.
7. You receive more direct visibility. Your guests enjoy a smoother experience.
   We grow together — and no money changes hands on either side.
8. It's free for travellers, and free for early villa partners. No commissions,
   no middlemen. Join the Other Bali villa partner network in minutes.

## Voiceover — Bahasa Indonesia (8 beats)

1. Other Bali adalah panduan Bali yang dikurasi oleh warga lokal — tempat yang
   tepat untuk momen Anda, dipilih dan dijelaskan oleh orang-orang yang
   benar-benar tinggal di sini.
2. Tanpa ulasan hasil salinan. Tanpa peringkat berbayar. Tanpa daftar bersponsor.
   Hanya rekomendasi lokal yang jujur dan tepercaya — dan bagi wisatawan, selalu
   gratis.
3. Saat seseorang menemukan vila Anda lewat Other Bali, mereka langsung menuju
   Anda — situs web, WhatsApp, halaman pemesanan Anda. Tanpa marketplace, tanpa
   perantara, tanpa komisi.
4. Vila Anda mendapat halamannya sendiri yang tampil indah — foto Anda, cerita
   Anda, tautan langsung Anda, dan panduan Bali pribadi yang benar-benar dipakai
   tamu selama menginap.
5. Sebagai gantinya, Anda cukup mengenalkan tamu Anda pada Other Bali — tambahkan
   tautan kami di situs Anda, sebut kami di Instagram, atau letakkan kode QR kami
   di kartu sambutan.
6. Bersama, kita membantu wisatawan menemukan Bali yang lebih baik — Anda
   mengirim tamu kepada kami, dan kami mengarahkan wisatawan langsung kepada Anda.
7. Anda mendapat lebih banyak keterlihatan langsung. Tamu Anda menikmati
   pengalaman yang lebih mulus. Kita tumbuh bersama — tanpa ada uang berpindah
   tangan di kedua sisi.
8. Gratis untuk wisatawan, dan gratis untuk mitra vila awal. Tanpa komisi, tanpa
   perantara. Bergabunglah dengan jaringan mitra vila Other Bali dalam hitungan
   menit.

## Visual scenario (shared across both languages)

1. **Opening.** A paper-collage map of Bali; hand-cut markers pop in — a coffee
   cup, a wave, a temple gate, a palm — a soft gouache sun at the horizon.
2. **Trust.** Cards for ratings / ads / sponsored lift up and drift away, leaving
   one warm card with a hand-drawn tick — resident-recommended.
3. **Direct bookings (the key shot).** A traveller's phone joins by one clean ink
   line — through the Other Bali emblem — straight to the villa (website · chat ·
   booking tokens). A "marketplace" slab tries to sit in the middle and is sliced
   clean in half; both halves drift away, the line unbroken.
4. **Villa page.** A villa page assembles — photo strip, title, description,
   action buttons, QR card — then the QR blooms into a fan of guide icons: food,
   spa, beach, surf, coffee, delivery.
5. **You introduce us.** Three tokens fly to the emblem — a website window, an
   Instagram-style camera glyph, a welcome card with a QR square.
6. **Two arrows (mutual).** The villa and the Other Bali emblem are joined by two
   curved ink arrows, both directions — we bring you guests, you recommend us.
7. **Hearts.** Villa → traveller → Bali, linked by two small ink hearts; the
   emblem warm behind them.
8. **Ending.** A calm closing frame — the emblem centered, a "free" ribbon and a
   frangipani; captions carry "free for travellers · free for early villa
   partners · no commissions, no middlemen · join in minutes".

## Production notes
- One style key locks the look; attached to every clip so all eight read as one
  film. Three beats (1 · 2 · 6) reuse earlier renders; five are new.
- Each voice take is fit to its fixed 10s block (centered; a small overrun is
  sped up pitch-safely).
- Assembled per language via the Higgsfield explainer workflow (per-block voice
  over per-block clip) with burned subtitles, then wired into the `/villas`
  "Villa film" block. English ships as `public/scenes/villas-film.mp4`; the
  Indonesian cut is kept alongside for the language toggle / founder use.

# Other Bali — Villa partnership film · script

**Purpose:** explain, on the `/villas` page, what Other Bali is and how the villa
partnership works — the essence of the product, why the two sides are useful to
each other, and the core principle that travellers are never routed through a
third party.
**Audience:** villa owners, managers and boutique-stay operators in Bali.
**Style:** mixed-media collage (non-photoreal), warm editorial — cream /
terracotta / teal / ink-brown, one locked style key.
**Voice:** warm, clear female narrator (Maya), English.
**Aspect / length:** 16:9 landscape · ~60s (six 10-second blocks). A 30s cut =
blocks **1 · 3 · 6**.
**Subtitles:** burned in (so it reads fine muted), handwritten paper-label font.

Guardrails honoured: no tourist payments, no marketplace/checkout, no "booking
volume" promise, no invented venue. Other Bali owns the decision, explanation and
trusted link; the villa owns hosting, booking, payment and the guest relationship.

---

## Full narration (read straight through)

> Other Bali is a resident-curated guide to the island — the right place for the
> moment you're in, chosen and explained by people who actually live here.
>
> Not scraped reviews, not ads, not a ranking you can buy — just honest local
> guidance travellers can trust. And for them, it's always free.
>
> When a traveller finds your villa, they reach you directly — your website, your
> WhatsApp, your booking page. No marketplace, no middleman, no commission.
>
> Your villa gets a real page with your own photos and links, direct discovery,
> and a personalised guide your guests will actually use.
>
> In return, you simply link us — on your site, on Instagram, and with our QR on
> your welcome card. That's the whole exchange.
>
> More guests for you, a better Bali for them — and no money changes hands, on
> either side. Add your villa in minutes.

## Block by block

### Block 1 — What Other Bali is
- **Voiceover:** "Other Bali is a resident-curated guide to the island — the
  right place for the moment you're in, chosen and explained by people who
  actually live here."
- **On screen:** a paper-collage map of Bali on cream; hand-cut markers — a
  coffee cup, a wave, a palm, a temple gate — pop into place; a soft gouache sun
  glows at the horizon. Slow push-in.
- **Sound:** warm ambient pad, faint paper rustle, light birdsong.

### Block 2 — Why it matters (the point of the product)
- **Voiceover:** "Not scraped reviews, not ads, not a ranking you can buy — just
  honest local guidance travellers can trust. And for them, it's always free."
- **On screen:** three cut-paper cards flip up — a crossed-out star/ratings card
  and a crossed-out "ad" tag fall away, leaving one warm card with a hand-drawn
  tick. Gentle settle.
- **Sound:** ambient pad, a soft paper flip, a light confirming chime.

### Block 3 — Direct, never through a third party
- **Voiceover:** "When a traveller finds your villa, they reach you directly —
  your website, your WhatsApp, your booking page. No marketplace, no middleman,
  no commission."
- **On screen:** a torn-paper traveller with a phone; a single clean ink arrow
  draws straight from the phone to a collage villa — no boxes or "marketplace"
  layer in between. Slow drift left to right.
- **Sound:** ambient pad, a light chime as the arrow connects.

### Block 4 — What we give the villa
- **Voiceover:** "Your villa gets a real page with your own photos and links,
  direct discovery, and a personalised guide your guests will actually use."
- **On screen:** a cut-paper villa page assembles — photo strip, link buttons,
  and a small QR guide card sliding in beside it. Elements snap into place.
- **Sound:** ambient pad, soft paper snaps as pieces land.

### Block 5 — What the villa gives us (mutual)
- **Voiceover:** "In return, you simply link us — on your site, on Instagram, and
  with our QR on your welcome card. That's the whole exchange."
- **On screen:** three small cut-paper tokens light up in turn — a website link,
  an Instagram glyph, a welcome/Wi-Fi card with a QR — each connected back to the
  warm Other Bali emblem by a short ink line.
- **Sound:** ambient pad, three light ticks.

### Block 6 — Both grow + call to action
- **Voiceover:** "More guests for you, a better Bali for them — and no money
  changes hands, on either side. Add your villa in minutes."
- **On screen:** the collage villa and the Other Bali emblem joined by two curved
  ink arrows looping both directions; a frangipani and a "free" paper tag settle
  in; warm closing frame.
- **Sound:** warm resolving ambient pad, a soft paper stamp.

## Production notes
- One style key locks the look; it is attached to every block so the six clips
  read as one film.
- Each voice take is ~8–9s to sit centered in its fixed 10s block.
- Assembled via the Higgsfield explainer workflow (per-block voice over per-block
  clip), then wired into the `/villas` "Villa film" block via
  `public/scenes/villas-film.mp4` (fetched at prebuild; poster fallback where the
  CDN is unreachable).

# First backlink wave — venue outreach

Goal: turn our existing editorial venue pages into **backlinks**. Every link
from a venue's own website to its Other Bali page is real domain authority we
can't get any other way, and it's fully inside our own relationships. This is
the single highest-ROI off-page move for a new domain (`docs/seo-strategy.md`
§3.C).

## The offer (honest framing)

We already wrote a free editorial feature on the venue. The message just tells
them it exists, gives them the link, and makes it easy to share. We do **not**
pay for links and we do **not** promise ranking in exchange — the link is a
natural consequence of a genuine feature, nothing more. (Guardrail #6: no paid
ranking. Also protects us from Google link-scheme penalties.)

Each venue's page URL is: `https://otherbali.com/places/<slug>`

## Who to contact first (priority order)

1. **Venues with their own website** — a link from a real site is worth far more
   than a social mention. From the recent research pass, these have official
   sites (best first targets): Jalapeño, Genius Cafe, Massimo, Gong, Kuu,
   Soul on the Beach, Ku De Ta, Corner House, Revolver, Ginger Moon, La Casetta,
   Mauri, Natys, 7AM Bakers, SAIA Wellness, You Spa, Poule de Luxe, Think Pink,
   plus the established Canggu/Ubud/Uluwatu venues that already have sites.
2. **Instagram-only venues** — still worth it: a Stories mention or a link in bio
   sends referral traffic and social signal, even if it's not a hard backlink.
3. Leave the weakly-sourced / aggregator-only ones for later.

## Template A — WhatsApp / Instagram DM (Bali venues live here)

> Personalise the bracketed bits. Keep it short — this is a chat, not an email.

Hi {Venue} team 👋 We run **Other Bali** (otherbali.com), a free guide that
points travellers to the right place for the moment they're in — no ads,
travellers never pay. We've featured you. Here's your page:
**otherbali.com/places/{slug}**

Nothing to sign, no cost. If you like it, feel free to share it with your guests
or add a link on your site — and if any detail (hours, a must-order dish) needs
fixing, just tell us and we'll update it the same day 🌴

## Template B — Email

**Subject:** {Venue} is featured on Other Bali (free)

Hi {name / {Venue} team},

We run **Other Bali** (https://otherbali.com) — a free trip-planning guide that
recommends Bali venues by the moment they suit. No ads, and travellers never pay.

We've written an editorial feature on **{Venue}**. You can see it here:
→ **https://otherbali.com/places/{slug}**

There's nothing to sign and no cost. If it reads well to you, two things would
genuinely help us keep sending you the right guests:

1. **Share the page** with your guests or on your socials.
2. **Add a link to it from your website** — a simple "As featured on Other Bali"
   line, or an entry on your press/links page, is perfect.

And if any detail is off — opening hours, a signature dish, anything — just
reply and we'll update it the same day.

Warmly,
Fadjri · Other Bali Team

## Personalisation tokens

- `{Venue}` — venue name.
- `{slug}` — the page slug (URL is `otherbali.com/places/{slug}`).
- Optional one-liner: drop in the specific thing we praised ("loved your
  wood-fired sourdough") — a 10-second personalisation roughly doubles reply
  rates. Pull it from the venue's `why_its_here` / `what_to_order`.

## Do / don't

- ✅ Genuine, warm, local, low-friction. One clear ask.
- ✅ Invite corrections — it opens the relationship (and later the TablePilot
   conversation) and keeps our data fresh via a real person, not scraping (#1).
- ❌ No payment for links. No "link to us and we'll rank you higher." No mass
   identical blast — personalise at least `{Venue}` + one detail.
- ❌ Don't over-send: one message, one polite follow-up a week later, then stop.

## Tracking (keep it simple)

A one-tab sheet: Venue · slug · contacted (date) · channel · replied · **linked
(y/n)** · notes. The only metric that matters is the *linked* column — that's
the backlink count. Re-check monthly; once GSC data flows, cross-reference with
the *Links* report (external links) in Search Console.

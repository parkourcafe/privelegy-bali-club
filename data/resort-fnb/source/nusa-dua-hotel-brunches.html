Nusa Dua hotel Sunday brunches

Every 5-star Sunday brunch in Nusa Dua, compared — price, what's included, pool access & how to book. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Nusa Dua's hotel Sunday brunches run from about IDR 650,000 to 1,650,000 per adult (non-alcoholic; free-flow alcohol adds roughly IDR 450,000–1,100,000). The best value is Apurva Kempinski's Brunchcation at IDR 798,000++ — it bundles the buffet plus pool, waterslide and beach access, so it doubles as a day pass. For the highest-end spread, Kayuputi at The St. Regis is the marquee (from IDR 1,250,000). Kids are typically half price and under-6 free. Most brunches run Sunday 11:00–15:00 and require a reservation.

Frequently asked

How much is Sunday brunch in Nusa Dua?

From about IDR 650,000 to 1,650,000 per adult for the food; free-flow alcohol adds roughly IDR 450,000–1,100,000. Children are usually half price and under-6 free.

Which Nusa Dua brunch includes pool access?

Apurva Kempinski's Brunchcation (IDR 798,000++) explicitly includes pool, waterslide and beach access. The Westin Ikan brunch includes pool & beach access too.

Do I need to book, and can non-hotel-guests come?

Yes on both — these brunches are open to outside guests and are reservation-only, especially in peak season. Book via the channel listed for each venue.

What's the most luxurious option?

Kayuputi at The St. Regis (from IDR 1,650,000 Sunday) is the highest-end, beachfront à-la-carte brunch.

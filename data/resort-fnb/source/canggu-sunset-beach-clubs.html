Canggu sunset beach clubs

Canggu's beach-club sunset scene — entry, table & daybed minimum spend, and how far ahead to book. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Canggu's sunset is beach-club-heavy and mostly free to enter with a redeemable table or daybed minimum spend, from about IDR 350,000 per person at a table to IDR 1.25–10 million for a VIP bed. Finns is the biggest (its own booking engine, no minimum spend), La Brisa and The Lawn are the boho sunset icons, COMO Beach Club is the luxury one and Atlas the mega-club.
The sunset rule. La Brisa and The Lawn stop same-day bookings around 15:30 and pre-booking is essential for a sunset table; deposits are non-refundable. Finns has no minimum spend but its premium daybeds book out — arrive before 14:00 to save up to 50%.

Frequently asked

How much is a Canggu beach club at sunset?

Mostly free entry with a redeemable table/daybed minimum spend — from about IDR 350,000 per person at a table to IDR 1.25–10 million for a VIP bed. Atlas adds a separate ~IDR 100,000 entry.

Which Canggu beach club has no minimum spend?

Finns Beach Club charges no entry and no minimum spend — you only pay for what you order; premium daybed packages are separate and book out.

How far ahead should I book for sunset?

La Brisa and The Lawn stop same-day bookings around 15:30, so reserve a sunset table 1–2 days ahead, especially on weekends.

Cheapest way in?

Vue Beach Club's Klook day pass (~US$11) for a pool day; Finns for free entry if you skip a daybed.

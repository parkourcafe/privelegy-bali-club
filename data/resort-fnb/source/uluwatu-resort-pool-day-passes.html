Uluwatu clifftop pool day passes

Cliff-edge infinity pools by the day — every priced pass and what the credit covers. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Uluwatu clifftop resort pool day passes run from about IDR 300,000 to 750,000 per adult (with Bulgari a separate luxury tier at IDR 5,000,000++ per couple). The best-value cliff pool is Six Senses (IDR 300,000++, most redeemable); the icon is The Edge's Oneeighty glass sky-pool (IDR 700,000); the only one live on the DayPass app is Anantara (IDR 500,000). These are pool passes — for the sunset-bar side of Uluwatu see the sunset-clubs guide.
Cliff pools sell out: Oneeighty's glass sky-pool and Alila's edge pool cap daily numbers and often reserve out on weekends — book a day or two ahead, and confirm how much of the price returns as F&B credit.

Frequently asked

How much is an Uluwatu clifftop pool day pass?

From about IDR 300,000 (Six Senses) to 750,000 (Alila Villas) per adult. The Edge's Oneeighty glass sky-pool is IDR 700,000; Bulgari's curated day is a separate IDR 5,000,000++ per couple.

Which Uluwatu pool is easiest to book online?

Anantara — it's the only Uluwatu clifftop resort live on the DayPass app (IDR 500,000). Others book by hotel email, Chope or concierge.

Is the price redeemable as food and drink?

Largely, yes — Six Senses (~230k credit), Oneeighty (600k credit) and Alila (350k credit) return much of the pass as F&B credit.

What's the difference from the sunset clubs?

These are daytime pool passes. For sunset drinks at Single Fin, El Kabron, Sundays and Savaya, see the Uluwatu sunset-clubs guide.

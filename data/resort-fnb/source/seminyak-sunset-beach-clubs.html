Seminyak sunset beach clubs

Where Bali's beach-club sunset was invented — entry, seat minimum spend and how to book a front-row daybed. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Seminyak invented the Bali beach-club sunset. Most are free to enter but charge a redeemable seat minimum spend, from about IDR 150,000 per person at a table to IDR 1.5–3.5 million for a sunset daybed or cabana. The icons are Potato Head and Ku De Ta; the cheapest front-row is La Plancha's beanbags on the sand; Mrs Sippy is one of the few with a real flat entry fee.
The sunset rule. Book a daybed 1–3 days ahead for weekends; the beanbag/walk-in spots (La Plancha, Cocoon) fill by ~16:30. Minimum spends are redeemable on F&B, not entry fees. Note: Café del Mar sits just north in Berawa (Canggu side), not central Seminyak.

Frequently asked

How much is a Seminyak beach club at sunset?

Most are free to enter but charge a redeemable seat minimum spend — roughly IDR 150,000 per person at a table up to IDR 1.5–3.5 million for a sunset daybed. Mrs Sippy is one of the few with a flat entry fee (IDR 150,000).

Which is the cheapest front-row sunset?

La Plancha's beanbags on the sand — walk-in with a small drink minimum. Cocoon has free entry too.

Do I need to book a daybed ahead?

Yes for weekends and at the icons (Potato Head, Ku De Ta) — 1–3 days ahead. Walk-in spots fill by around 16:30.

Is Café del Mar in Seminyak?

It sits just north in Berawa (the Canggu side), a short drive from central Seminyak — included here for planning.

A Bali resort Sunday brunch is a whole afternoon — buffet or à la carte, usually with pool or beach access, from about IDR 400,000 per adult (COMO Uma in Ubud) to the grand seafood spreads at St Regis and Mulia in Nusa Dua (IDR 1.1–1.65M). Free-flow tiers push higher. Below is every district with a brunch scene; open a guide for the full priced list. Most take outside guests by reservation and run roughly Sunday 12:00–15:30.
Reserve by Thursday. The popular resort brunches — St Regis, Kempinski, W Bali, Four Seasons Sundara — sell out for Sunday, especially in high season. Book mid-week and ask what's non-alcoholic vs free-flow, since that's the biggest price swing.

Frequently asked

How much is a hotel Sunday brunch in Bali?

From about IDR 400,000 per adult (COMO Uma, Ubud) to IDR 1.1–1.65M for the grand seafood brunches at St Regis and Mulia in Nusa Dua. Most beachfront resort brunches sit around IDR 550,000–950,000, with free-flow tiers higher.

Which district has the most brunch choice?

Nusa Dua, with 14 resort brunches — from Kempinski's Brunchcation (IDR 798,000) to St Regis' Kayuputi (IDR 1.25–1.65M). Sanur and Ubud are quieter and better value.

Do I need to book ahead?

Yes — the popular ones (St Regis, Kempinski, W Bali, Four Seasons Sundara) sell out for Sunday in high season. Reserve by mid-week and check non-alcoholic vs free-flow pricing.

Which is the best value?

COMO Uma Cucina in Ubud at IDR 400,000, and The Meru's family brunch in Sanur at IDR 550,000.

Seminyak hotel Sunday brunches

Beachfront resort brunches — price, free-flow tiers and how to book. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Seminyak's hotel Sunday brunches run from about IDR 650,000 to 950,000 per adult (non-alcoholic), with champagne tiers up to ~IDR 3,200,000. The value pick is Alila's Seasalt coastal brunch (IDR 650,000); the party one is W Bali's Starfish Bloo (IDR 950,000). (Courtyard runs a Saturday BBQ from IDR 425,000 — not a Sunday brunch.)

Frequently asked

How much is Sunday brunch in Seminyak?

From about IDR 650,000 (Alila Seasalt) to 950,000 (W Bali) per adult; champagne free-flow tiers run up to ~IDR 3,200,000.

Which is best for a party brunch?

W Bali's Starfish Bloo — buffet, pool access and free-flow tiers with the W's DJ energy.

Any cheaper hotel option?

Courtyard's Saturday Backyard BBQ from IDR 425,000 — an evening BBQ rather than a Sunday brunch.

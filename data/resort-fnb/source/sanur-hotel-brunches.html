Sanur hotel Sunday brunches

The quiet side's beachfront brunches — price, what's included and how to book. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Sanur's hotel Sunday brunches run from about IDR 550,000 to 1,250,000 per adult. The newest flagship is The Meru's family brunch at Arunika (IDR 550,000); the design-led beachfront option is Andaz's at Fisherman's Club (IDR 590,000). Most run Sunday around 12:00–15:00 and take outside guests by reservation. (Mercure runs a themed all-you-can-eat dinner buffet from IDR 198,000 — not a Sunday brunch, but the cheapest hotel buffet in Sanur.)

Frequently asked

How much is Sunday brunch in Sanur?

From about IDR 550,000 to 1,250,000 per adult. The Meru (IDR 550,000) and Andaz (IDR 590,000) are the mid-range picks; kids are usually half price.

Which Sanur brunch is best for families?

The Meru's Arunika family brunch (live music, poolside) and Andaz at Fisherman's Club (pool access, beachfront).

Is there a cheap hotel buffet in Sanur?

Mercure's themed all-you-can-eat dinner buffet from IDR 198,000 — it's an evening buffet, not a Sunday brunch, but the best-value hotel buffet in Sanur.

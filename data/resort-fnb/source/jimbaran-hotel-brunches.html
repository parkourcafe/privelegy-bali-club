Jimbaran hotel Sunday brunches

Resort brunches on the bay — price, pool access and how to book. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Jimbaran's resort Sunday brunches run from about IDR 650,000 to 1,250,000 per adult, and most bundle pool or jacuzzi access. The value pick is InterContinental's tiered Sunday brunch + pool (from IDR 650,000) or Movenpick's (IDR 700,000, with pool & jacuzzi); the flagship is Four Seasons' Sundara “Pink” beach brunch (IDR 1,250,000).

Frequently asked

How much is Sunday brunch in Jimbaran?

From about IDR 650,000 (InterContinental) to 1,250,000 (Four Seasons Sundara) per adult; most include pool access.

Which brunch doubles as a pool day?

InterContinental's tiers and Movenpick's Anarasa both bundle pool (and jacuzzi at Movenpick) with the brunch.

What's the flagship?

Four Seasons' Sundara beachfront “Pink” Sunday brunch (IDR 1,250,000).

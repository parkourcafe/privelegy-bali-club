Ubud hotel Sunday brunches

Valley- and river-view resort brunches — price, what's included and how to book. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Ubud's resort Sunday brunches run from about IDR 400,000 to 850,000 per adult. The best value is COMO Uma's Italian brunch at Uma Cucina (IDR 400,000); the wellness pick is COMO Shambhala's organic “Glow” brunch (IDR 650,000); the grand one is Mandapa's Royal Brunch on the Ayung valley (IDR 850,000). Four Seasons Sayan runs a riverside farmer's brunch (price on request).

Frequently asked

How much is Sunday brunch in Ubud?

From about IDR 400,000 (COMO Uma Cucina) to 850,000 (Mandapa) per adult. COMO Shambhala's organic Glow brunch is IDR 650,000.

Which Ubud brunch is best for wellness?

COMO Shambhala's “Glow” — an organic, homegrown-produce brunch at the wellness estate.

Which has the best view?

Mandapa's Sawah Terrace over the Ayung valley, and Four Seasons Sayan's riverside setting.

Nusa Dua resort day passes

Use a five-star pool & beach for the day — every priced pass, and the ones you must book direct. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Nusa Dua resort day passes run from about IDR 150,000 to 1,700,000 per adult. The cheapest real pass is Ayodya (IDR 150,000 net); the best-value pool day is Merusaka (IDR 200,000 net); the most-searched is Club Med's all-inclusive day (from ~AUD 128). Note: The Apurva Kempinski's Reef Beach Club is free to enter (book ahead), and Ayodya, Merusaka, Nusa Dua Beach Hotel and Hilton sell direct only — they are not on Klook or the DayPass app.
Book these direct, not on an app: Ayodya, Merusaka, Nusa Dua Beach Hotel and Hilton take day-pass bookings only by WhatsApp or their own site — no aggregator markup. Sofitel, Westin and Club Med are aggregator-listed; compare the direct price before booking.

Frequently asked

How much is a Nusa Dua day pass?

From IDR 150,000 (Ayodya) to 1,700,000 (Hilton) per adult. The best-value pool day is Merusaka at IDR 200,000 net; Club Med's all-inclusive day (~AUD 128) is the most searched.

Which Nusa Dua resorts must I book direct?

Ayodya, Merusaka, Nusa Dua Beach Hotel and Hilton sell day passes only via WhatsApp or their own site — they are not on Klook or the DayPass app. Booking direct avoids aggregator markup.

Is the Apurva Kempinski beach club free?

Yes — the Reef Beach Club has free entry and free use of the pool and loungers with no minimum spend, confirmed for 2026. The catch is you now need to reserve in advance, and premium cabanas may carry a minimum or deposit.

Which is cheapest?

Ayodya at IDR 150,000 net per adult, followed by Merusaka at IDR 200,000.

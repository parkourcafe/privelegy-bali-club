Jimbaran resort day passes

Bay-front resort pools & beaches by the day — every priced pass and what the credit covers. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Jimbaran resort day passes run from about IDR 211,750 to 2,500,000 per adult (the top figure is Sundara's daybed for two). The value entry is Vivara Bali (from IDR 211,750) or Le Meridien (about US$20); the mid pick is InterContinental (IDR 500,000, half back as credit); the flagship is Four Seasons' Sundara beach club. Note AYANA sells no day pass — its pools are for in-house guests and restaurant bookings only.
AYANA has no day pass: the only way onto AYANA/RIMBA grounds for the day is a restaurant or Rock Bar reservation, not a pool pass — a common tourist mix-up. For a pool day, use InterContinental, Le Meridien or Sundara.

Frequently asked

How much is a Jimbaran resort day pass?

From about IDR 211,750 (Vivara) or US$20 (Le Meridien) to Sundara's US$46.95 lunch pass and IDR 2,500,000 daybed for two. InterContinental's is IDR 500,000 with half back as credit.

Can I get a day pass at AYANA?

No — AYANA sells no pool day pass. You can only access the grounds by booking a restaurant or Rock Bar reservation.

Which is best value?

Vivara Bali from IDR 211,750, or Le Meridien's sky-lagoon pass at about US$20.

Which is the flagship beach day?

Four Seasons' Sundara beach club — a lunch pass from US$46.95 or a daybed for two at IDR 2,500,000++.

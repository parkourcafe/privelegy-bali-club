Uluwatu sunset clubs

Every clifftop sunset club on the Bukit, compared — entry, seat minimum spend, and exactly how far ahead to book a front-row table. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Uluwatu's cliff clubs are mostly free (or cheap) to enter, but charge a redeemable minimum spend by seat — roughly IDR 400,000 per person at a table up to IDR 1.5–10 million for a sunset daybed or cabana, all spent on food and drinks. The icon is Single Fin (Suluban, legendary Sunday sessions); the show-stopper is One Eighty at The Edge (glass sky-pool 162 m above the sea); the white-sand cove is Sundays Beach Club. The one rule that matters: for a front-row sunset seat, book days ahead and arrive by ~16:30.
The sunset rule. Front-row seats sell out and many clubs stop same-day bookings by mid-afternoon. Reserve 2–7 days ahead for weekends and full-moon dates, arrive by 16:30–17:00, and check the seat minimum spend before you go — it's redeemable on F&B, not an entry fee. Adults-only at several (Savaya, El Kabron, Ulu Cliffhouse).

One Eighty at The Edge the view

Glass sky-pool, 162 m above the sea · Pecatu

The most photographed sunset spot on the Bukit: a cantilevered glass-bottom pool over the cliff. General admission is IDR 700,000, of which IDR 600,000 comes back as an F&B credit; VIP beds and cabanas go up from IDR 3,000,000. Sold online (non-refundable), so lock a sunset slot in advance.

Sundays Beach Club

Frequently asked

How much does an Uluwatu sunset club cost?

Most are free or cheap to enter but charge a redeemable minimum spend by seat — roughly IDR 400,000 per person at a table, up to IDR 1.5–10 million for a sunset daybed or cabana, all spent on food and drinks. Sundays and Karma are IDR 800,000 including a IDR 500,000 credit.

Which is the best Uluwatu sunset spot?

For the view, One Eighty's glass sky-pool at The Edge; for atmosphere, Single Fin; for a beach, Sundays Beach Club's white-sand cove with its 18:30 bonfire.

Do I need to book, and how far ahead?

Yes for a front-row sunset seat — reserve 2–7 days ahead for weekends and full-moon dates, and arrive by 16:30–17:00. Several clubs stop same-day bookings by mid-afternoon.

Are Uluwatu sunset clubs family-friendly?

Sundays Beach Club and Palmilla are; Savaya, El Kabron and Ulu Cliffhouse are adults-only.

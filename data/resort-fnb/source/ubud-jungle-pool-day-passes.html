Ubud jungle pool day passes

Swim over the rainforest for the day — every priced pass and what the credit covers. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Ubud jungle-pool day passes run from about IDR 150,000 to 1,028,500 per adult, and most prices are redeemable as F&B credit, so the real cost is close to what you'd eat anyway. The cheapest five-star pass is The Westin Ubud (IDR 150,000, fully creditable); the icon is Alila Ubud's green pool (IDR 350,000); the design flagship is Bambu Indah (from IDR 1,028,500).
“Net cost” tip: where the pass is fully redeemable as F&B credit (Westin Ubud, Kappa Senses, Kayon Jungle), a lunch-and-drinks day costs you little more than the food itself — the pool comes effectively free.

Frequently asked

How much is an Ubud pool day pass?

From IDR 150,000 (Westin Ubud) to about 1,028,500 (Bambu Indah) per adult. Alila Ubud's iconic green pool is IDR 350,000.

Is the day-pass price redeemable?

At many resorts, yes — Westin Ubud, Kappa Senses and Kayon Jungle credit the pass fully against food and drinks, so the pool is effectively free if you eat there.

Which is the famous green pool?

Alila Ubud's edge pool over the Ayung valley — the most photographed in Ubud — at IDR 350,000.

Which is best for families?

Padma Resort Ubud has the longest pool in Ubud and a strong kids' setup; the day pass is IDR 500,000 (child 350,000).

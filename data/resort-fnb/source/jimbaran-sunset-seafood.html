Jimbaran sunset seafood

Grilled seafood on the sand as the sun goes down — set prices, where to sit and how to get a front-row table. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Jimbaran's sunset is grilled seafood eaten at tables on the sand. Set dinners run roughly IDR 120,000–500,000 per person at the beach warungs (Teba, Bawang Merah, Menega), with 2-person sets to IDR 1.5–2.6M. Above the beach, AYANA's Rock Bar is the cliff sunset icon (seat minimum spend from IDR 300,000) and Sundara at Four Seasons is the polished beach club. Front-row sand tables at sunset are scarce — arrive by 16:30 or WhatsApp ahead.
The sunset rule. The seafood warungs are walk-in but front-row tables on the sand go fast around 16:30–17:00 — WhatsApp to hold one. AYANA keeps 16:00–17:00 at Rock Bar for hotel guests; non-guests are seated from 19:30, so for the actual sunset drink book early or pick a beach warung.

Frequently asked

How much is a Jimbaran seafood dinner?

Set menus run about IDR 120,000–500,000 per person; two-person seafood sets go up to IDR 1.5–2.6 million. Drinks, tax and service extra.

How do I get a front-row table on the sand?

Arrive by 16:30–17:00 or WhatsApp ahead — front-row sunset tables are limited. Formal online booking is thin at the warungs.

Can I watch sunset from Rock Bar as a non-guest?

The 16:00–17:00 sunset slot is for AYANA guests; non-guests are seated from 19:30. Book far ahead or choose a beach warung for the sunset itself.

Which is best value?

Teba Cafe's set (~US$13) is the cheapest bookable; Bawang Merah has the clearest published prices.

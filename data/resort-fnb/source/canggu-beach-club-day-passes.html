Canggu beach-club & pool day passes

Hotel beach clubs and pools by the day — price, credit and how to book. Prices checked 19 Jul 2026 · resident-curated, no paid placement.

Canggu's hotel day passes run from about IDR 175,000 to 800,000 per adult. The value pick is Vue Beach Club (~IDR 175,000); the boutique pick is Swarga Berawa (IDR 350,000 net); the luxury one is COMO Uma's beach club (IDR 500,000) or full-resort pass (IDR 800,000). Note Canggu's giant beach clubs — FINNS, Atlas, Vue — mostly run their own entry/daybed systems rather than a hotel pass.
Hotel pass vs beach-club daybed: COMO Uma and Swarga sell a true hotel day pass (fixed price, often creditable). The mega beach clubs (FINNS, Atlas Beach Fest) use their own ticketing and daybed minimums — different product, booked on their own engines or Klook.

Frequently asked

How much is a Canggu beach-club day pass?

From about IDR 175,000 (Vue Beach Club) to 800,000 (COMO Uma full-resort pass) per adult. Swarga Berawa is IDR 350,000 net.

What about FINNS and Atlas?

Canggu's mega beach clubs — FINNS, Atlas Beach Fest, Vue — run their own entry and daybed systems rather than a hotel day pass; book on their own engines or Klook.

Which is the luxury option?

COMO Uma Canggu — the only true luxury resort here — with a IDR 500,000 beach-club pass or IDR 800,000 full-resort pass (child 250,000).

Which is cheapest?

Vue Beach Club at roughly IDR 175,000 (about US$11) via Klook.

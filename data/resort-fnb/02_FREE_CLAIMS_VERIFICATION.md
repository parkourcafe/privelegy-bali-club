# "FREE / NO MINIMUM" CLAIMS — VERIFICATION RESULTS (19 Jul 2026)
Every place a page said "free entry" or "no minimum spend" was re-checked against the venue's own site/booking engine plus 2025–2026 reviews. **15 venues checked. 3 were wrong and are corrected; 4 were imprecise and are tightened; 8 held up.** All pages updated.

## ❌ WAS WRONG — corrected
- **Cocoon (Seminyak)** — page said "no published daybed minimum." **False.** Cocoon publishes a pool-daybed minimum from **IDR 500,000** (redeemable). Entry itself is free. → fixed.
- **El Kabron (Uluwatu)** — page implied "free, seat minimum ~IDR 350,000." **The 350k is the pool amenities fee, not the seat minimum.** Seating is reservation-only with a minimum of ~IDR 400k/pax (restaurant) up to **~850k–1M/pax for sunset cliff seats**. → fixed on the free-beach-clubs and Uluwatu sunset pages.
- **Savaya (Uluwatu)** — sunset page said "entry IDR 150,000." **Outdated.** Savaya is always ticketed; current entry runs **~IDR 300,000–900,000 by event**. Never free. → fixed.

## ⚠️ WAS IMPRECISE — tightened
- **Apurva Kempinski Reef (Nusa Dua)** — the earlier "free walk-in pool" was the big one Robert flagged. Verified: free entry and free pool/lounger use **is** real and current (no minimum on loungers) — **but advance reservation is now required**, so it is not a walk-in, and premium cabanas may carry a minimum. → corrected on all three pages it appears (free-beach-clubs, Nusa Dua day-passes, day-pass pillar).
- **W Bali WooBar (Seminyak)** — daybed minimum was understated at "~IDR 1,000,000." Actual **~IDR 2,000,000++** (up to ~2.8M). Bar walk-in is genuinely free. → fixed.
- **Hotel Indigo SugarSand (Seminyak)** — "walk-in pool free" was too generous; the most recent review says **pool use needs a purchase** (towel/F&B). Daybed minimum IDR 400,000. → softened.
- **Sofitel Manarai Beach House (Nusa Dua)** — my checklist had over-restricted it ("daybed carries a minimum"); most 2025–26 sources list it as **free entry AND no minimum** (rare). Event days are ticketed. → softened to "generally no minimum, confirm event days."

## ✅ HELD UP — accurate as written
- **Segara Village · Byrd House (Sanur)** — free entry, no minimum (small towel fee). Confirmed genuinely free.
- **Potato Head (Seminyak)** — free most of day; IDR 200k redeemable sunset cover 16:00–18:30 (matches the venue's own site).
- **FINNS (Canggu)** — free to walk in and use general seating with no minimum; only reserved daybeds carry a minimum (~IDR 1.1M+). Wording clarified so "no minimum" no longer reads as applying to daybeds.
- **La Brisa (Canggu)** — no entry; free first-come tables; premium tables from ~IDR 700k.
- **The Lawn (Canggu)** — no entry; free lounge seating; reserved seats carry a minimum.
- **Single Fin (Uluwatu)** — no entry; bar/general seating no minimum; the free sunset view is real (come early Sunday).
- **Ulu Cliffhouse (Uluwatu)** — free entry + walk-in bar; daybeds carry a minimum (~700k weekday / 1.2M weekend).
- **Atlas (Canggu)** — correctly shown as paid entry (~IDR 100–150k, non-redeemable) + redeemable seat minimum.

## The pattern (and the rule going forward)
The safe, true statement is: **commercial beach clubs are free to walk into and sit in general/bar seating, but any daybed or table carries a redeemable minimum spend; hotel pools generally need a booking and a minimum.** The two genuinely-free-with-no-minimum exceptions we could confirm are **Segara Byrd House** and **Kempinski Reef** (Reef now needs a reservation). Everything on the pages now reflects this, and the checklist rows for these 15 venues are marked ✅ VERIFIED with sources.

# Publication gates

A page may be `READY_FOR_CODEX_DRAFT` only when every applicable gate passes.

1. **Repository:** existing owners, aliases, canonicals, sitemap and internal links are inventoried.
2. **Intent:** the user job and one owner URL are explicit; no unresolved cannibalization.
3. **Evidence:** every planned factual claim has an approved ledger row and resolvable source.
4. **Entity:** name, type and geographic identity are adequate for the planned copy.
5. **Volatility:** operational facts have a refresh rule or are omitted.
6. **Owner:** no planned claim requires unanswered owner confirmation.
7. **Field:** no planned claim requires unfinished physical verification.
8. **Maps:** the action layer uses only confirmed entity handoffs; otherwise omit it or hold the claim/page.
9. **Editorial:** wording stays within allowed language and does not convert interpretation into fact.
10. **Technical:** unique metadata/canonical/intent, valid schema, SSR HTML, crawlable links, robots/index intent and sitemap state agree.
11. **Quality:** no thin clone, unsupported superlative, paid ranking or hidden conflict.
12. **Independent review:** reviewer signs off or leaves explicit unresolved blockers.

`HOLD` is topic- or claim-scoped. A held operational detail does not block a safe page whose brief explicitly omits it.
